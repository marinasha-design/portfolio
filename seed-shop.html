tasks UX research, wireframing, prototyping

Best Seeds is a local seed store application that allows users to order seeds with delivery, but also with a ready-made order in the store. With the ability to switch between multiple languages, the application allows users who do not speak a language (foreigners) to make purchases without a language barrier.

The Problem:

Language barrier of those who moved to a new place of residence. The inconvenience of mothers with prams and babies visiting an offline store.

The Goal:

Design an application that will allow mothers of babies to order goods without having to visit an offline store, as well as with support for several languages.
